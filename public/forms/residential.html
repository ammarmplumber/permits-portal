2. New Construction Details

Required Documents:

  • Complete structural plans
  • Foundation plans
  • MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) plans
  • Site plan showing setbacks and easements

2. Addition Details

Note: If your addition affects load-bearing walls, an engineering letter will be required.

Required Documents:

  • Floor plans
  • Site plan
  • Engineering letter (if load-bearing walls affected)
  • Structural details

2. Remodel Details

Note: A permit is only required for remodels if:

  • Project cost exceeds $10,000
  • Walls are being removed
  • Room use is changing

Required Documents:

  • Floor plans showing before/after (if walls changing)
  • Description of changes

2. Electrical Details

Note: Homeowners must pass a test to obtain an electrical permit for DIY work.

Required Documents:

  • Electrical diagram (for major work)
  • Electrician license information (or proof of homeowner test)

2. Plumbing Details

Note: A licensed plumber is typically required for most plumbing work.

Required Documents:

  • Plumbing diagram (for major work)
  • Contractor license if required

2. HVAC Details

Required Documents:

  • HVAC equipment specifications
  • Layout of ducts (if new or modified)

2. Accessory Building Details

Required Documents:

  • Site plan (showing distance from property lines)
  • Building plans if over 200 sq ft

2. Swimming Pool Details

Required Documents:

  • Site plan showing pool location, fencing/barrier
  • Manufacturer specs (if above-ground)
  • Electrical details (for pumps, etc.)

2. Storm Shelter Details

Required Documents:

  • Manufacturer specs or engineering plans
  • Site plan (if separate from main dwelling)

2. Solar PV Details

Required Documents:

  • Panel specifications
  • Structural assessment (roof) or site plan (ground)
  • Electrical line diagram

2. Generator Details

Required Documents:

  • Manufacturer specifications
  • Site plan showing generator location

2. Irrigation Details

Required Documents:

  • System layout
  • Backflow prevention details

2. Fence Details

Required Documents:

  • Site plan or property survey showing fence location

2. Roof Details

Required Documents:

  • Manufacturer specifications

2. Window/Door Replacement Details

Required Documents:

  • Window/door specifications
  • Engineering if altering structural openings

2. Concrete Details

Required Documents:

  • Site plan showing area of concrete

Additional Documents
